About Signovate

Project Overview

Signovate is an AI-powered sign language recognition system designed to break communication barriers for the deaf and hard of hearing community. Our technology uses computer vision and deep learning to translate American Sign Language (ASL) gestures into text and speech in real-time.

Key Features

Real-Time Processing

Our system processes sign language in real-time with minimal latency, enabling fluid conversation.

High Accuracy

Advanced neural networks ensure high recognition accuracy across a wide range of signing styles.

ASL Support

Currently supports American Sign Language with plans to expand to other sign languages.

Speech Output

Converts recognized signs into audible speech, enabling two-way communication.

Our Technology

Signovate uses a combination of computer vision techniques and deep learning models to detect hand movements, facial expressions, and body posture. Our system:

  • Identifies key points on the hands and fingers
  • Tracks movement patterns over time
  • Translates gestures into words and sentences
  • Continuously improves through machine learning

Our Mission

We believe that technology should be inclusive and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to break down communication barriers and create a more inclusive world where deaf and hard of hearing individuals can communicate freely and effortlessly with the hearing community.

Empowering communication through AI-powered sign language recognition technology. Breaking barriers and creating inclusive spaces for the deaf and hard of hearing community.
